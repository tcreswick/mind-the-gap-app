    📊 Why this site exists

    The official datasets are split by reporting year, which makes historical company analysis awkward. This viewer brings all available years together under one employer profile (linked by EmployerID), making trend analysis and at-a-glance gender split easier.

    Source data comes from the UK Government Gender Pay Gap Service.

    Gender pay gap reporting started in 2017 under regulations requiring larger employers (typically 250+ employees) to publish annual pay and bonus gap figures plus pay quartile splits. Deadlines are generally April (private/voluntary sector) and March (public sector).
